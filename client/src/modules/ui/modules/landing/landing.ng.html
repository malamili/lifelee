Know your air.

Comprehensive management solution for occupancy and carbon dioxide in your living spaces.

The challenge with indoor air

Carbon Dioxide is abundant in the spaces we live and has been given little attention in indoor air quality concerns. Recent research has shown that carbon dioxide concentrations above 500ppm dramatically decreases the cognitive abilities of the occupants of the space.

Air and occupancy

One of the primary contributor to high carbon dioxide concentration is occupancy of spaces. Lifelee aggregates carbon dioxide data from all your facilities using mobile, flexible internet of things technology, and enables the comparison of this data to the occupancy. Owners or managers of spaces are thus able to manage and redistribute the occupants of their spaces to improve health and productivity.

Predictive solution

Lifelee is a provident, suggestive system, that alerts the user when carbon dioxide levels are high. This enables the management on mass- and/or small-scale, identifying under/over-occupied indoor spaces. Lifelee is ultimately a decision support system for improving indoor quality of life and thus indoor value creation.
